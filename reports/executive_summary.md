# Executive Summary

This portfolio case study analyzes 65,752 orders and 180,519 order items from the DataCo supply chain dataset. It combines order-level delivery metrics with order-item sales and profit measures so logistics performance can be read alongside commercial exposure.

Headline results:
- Net sales: $33,054,402.4
- Total profit: $3,966,903.0
- Profit margin: 12.0%
- Late delivery rate: 54.8%
- Average shipping delay: 0.6 days
- Cancellation rate: 2.1%

At a decision level, the analysis points to two monitoring needs: late-delivery performance should be tracked across shipping mode and geography, and sales concentration should be reviewed with profit margin rather than revenue alone.
