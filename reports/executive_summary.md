# Executive Summary

This portfolio case study analyzes 65,752 orders and 180,519 order items from the DataCo supply chain dataset. The project focuses on delivery performance, commercial exposure, profitability, and dashboard-ready KPI governance.

Headline results:
- Net sales: $33,054,402.4
- Total profit: $3,966,903.0
- Profit margin: 12.0%
- Late delivery rate: 54.8%
- Average shipping delay: 0.6 days
- Cancellation rate: 2.1%

The main operational takeaway is that delivery risk should be managed at the intersection of shipping mode, market, and region. The main commercial takeaway is that category and regional sales should be interpreted with profitability context, because high sales do not always imply strong margin.
